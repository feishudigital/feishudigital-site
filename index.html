About Feishu Digital

Feishu Digital is an independent software studio based in Hamburg, Germany. We build tools that help professionals work more efficiently — without compromising on privacy or simplicity.

All our browser extensions process data locally. We don't run analytics, don't track users, and don't sell data. Our business model is simple: build great tools and charge a fair price.

Privacy First
